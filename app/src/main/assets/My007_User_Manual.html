My007 APP User Instruction Manual

 

1. My007 APP first time installation

1.1. Download My007 APP from the Android Play Store and install.  (Note: This APP currently does not support iOS mobile phones.)

1.2. For the first time installation, required to set a 4-digit password and remember the password.  Next setup the fingerprint authentication for unlocking the My007 APP.  (Note: This APP currently does not support face recognition.)

1.3. Insert the Libra USB A connector to the power adapter, and the USB Type C cable into the mobile phone. Slide the switch on the Libra to the alarm state.

1.4. My007 APP and Libra pairing for the first time: The My007 APP will auto-search Bluetooth device. Select My007 device in the My007 APP Bluetooth list and click to pair the connection.  (Note: If My007 APP and the Libra cannot be paired, turn OFF the mobile phone Bluetooth and ON again. Then go the My007 APP Bluetooth list and pair it with the Libra again.)

 

2. Protecting the mobile phone

2.1. During the mobile phone charging, the mobile phone is connected to the Libra and power adapter with the My007 APP running.

2.2. When the alarm cable is unplugged from the mobile phone, the Libra will trigger off an alarm. The My007 APP will also alarm.  Access the My007 APP using password or fingerprint to deactivate the alarm of the My007 APP and Libra.  (Note: Ensure the switch on the Libra is at the alarm state when charging the mobile phone in a public place.  Otherwise both the My007 App and Libra will not alarm off.)

 

3. One click to find the mobile phone

3.1. Press the button on the Libra for 2 seconds, and the mobile phone will trigger off an alarm.

3.2. Access the My007 APP using password or fingerprint to deactivate the alarm of the mobile phone.

 

4. Taking Selfie

4.1. Go to the mobile phone Bluetooth list and pair the mobile phone with the Libra.

4.2. Go to the mobile phone camera, and you can remotely take a selfie using the button on the Libra.

4.3. The Libra can be used to take selfie for distance up to 10 meters.

 

5. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

5.1. How do I unlock a locked screen?

On the lock screen page, enter the correct password to unlock the screen lock, and then click Unlock to access the APP.

 

5.2. What if I forget my password?

Press the Home button of the mobile phone 2 times to turn off this APP and this will also to deactivate the alarming.  Access the My007 APP, click on the parameter setting to reset the password. Or reinstall the My007 APP again.

 

5.3. No sound when alarming              

Check if the earphone is plugged to the mobile phone or not. Earphone is not recommended when the My007 APP is running as the alarm volume setting become low when this APP detects earphone mode.  This is to protect user with the earphone when the alarm is triggered off.

Check the switch on the Libra is at the alarm state or not.

My007 APP详细使用说明

 

一． APP首次使用

（1）在安卓应用商店下载My007 APP，安装；

（2）首次使用需要设置4位数字的密码，密码需要牢记；同时需要授权手机解锁的指纹密码可以解锁，APP暂不支持人脸识别解锁。

（3）将Libra报警线A公端插入电源适配器，Type C端插入手机，滑动开关滑到报警状态；

（4）APP和Libra首次连接，APP自动搜素蓝牙设备，在APP弹出的蓝牙列表中选择My007的设备，点击进行配对连接；首次配对连接完成后，以后设备会自动和APP连接

备注：首次配对连接，若APP出现和Libra不能连上的情况，请检查手机的蓝牙列表，如果蓝牙列表中出现Libra和手机配对连接，取消配对连接。待APP和Libra连接完成后，手机端可以再配对连接。

 

二．报警解锁

（1）警戒状态下，APP打开且和Libra连接良好；

（2）从手机中拔出报警线，Libra蜂鸣器响，手机报警，进入APP，录入指纹或输入密码，可以同时给APP和Libra解除报警；

备注：Libra报警按键状态在不报警位置时，手机APP和Libra都不报警。

 

三． 一键找手机

（1） 长按找手机按键2秒，APP报警，报警声响

（2） 进入APP，录入指纹或输入密码，APP解除报警；

 

四． 自拍

       （1）在手机蓝牙列表中，Libra和手机做蓝牙配对

（2）手机进入相机，按Libra拍照键，可以遥控自拍

（3） 遥控自拍的距离最远10米

 

五． 使用问题汇总

（1）出现锁屏页后如何解锁？ 

在锁屏的页面上，输入正确密码解屏锁，再点击进入APP，解锁

（2）忘记解锁密码怎么办？

按2次手机Home键，关闭本APP，即可关闭报警铃声。再次进入APP后，点击参数设置，重新设置解锁密码。或重装APP，重新建立Libra和APP的配对连接。

 （3）听不到警报铃声是怎么回事？                                   

检查是否是插上了耳机。当打开警戒模式后，不推荐使用耳机。因为本APP检测到耳机模式时，警报铃声音量设置的比较低。

（4）检查Libra是否开启警戒模式， 并且触发警报
